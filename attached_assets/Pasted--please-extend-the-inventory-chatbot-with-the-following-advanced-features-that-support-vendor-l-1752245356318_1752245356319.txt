
>please extend the inventory chatbot with the following **advanced features** that support vendor-level decision-making and operational insights.

> ### 📊 **Analytics & Reporting Enhancements**
>
> The chatbot should intelligently process vendor queries related to business performance and generate contextual answers or suggestions.
>
> #### ➕ Include the following logistics and inventory formulas:
>
> * **Inventory Turnover Ratio** = COGS / Average Inventory
> * **Stock Cover Duration** = Inventory / Daily Sales Avg
> * **Lead Time Demand** = Lead Time × Average Daily Usage
> * **Economic Order Quantity (EOQ)**
> * **Safety Stock** = (Max Daily Usage × Max Lead Time) – (Avg Daily Usage × Avg Lead Time)
> * **Fill Rate** = (Orders Fulfilled / Total Orders) × 100
> * **Shrinkage Rate** = (Recorded Inventory - Actual Inventory) / Recorded Inventory
>
> These should be integrated into the analytics engine so the bot can answer questions like:
>
> * “How often am I turning over stock?”
> * “Do I have enough safety stock for flea shampoo?”
> * “What’s my ideal reorder quantity?”
>
> #### ➕ Advanced Inventory Intelligence:
>
> * Show **dead stock alerts** based on stagnation.
> * Surface **top 3 selling products** by volume, revenue, and margin.
> * Provide **cost-to-sale ratio** analytics.
> * Add **product sales breakdowns** by category.
>

> ### 💬 **Natural Language Prompt Handling Enhancements**
>
> Improve the bot’s flexibility in recognizing vague or business-oriented queries, such as:
>
> * “Am I doing well this month?”
> * “What’s dragging my sales down?”
> * “Do I need to restock anything?”
> * “How much am I making on average per item?”
>
> These should be interpreted using **intent recognition and vector similarity fallback**, triggering proper analytic responses using DB data and formulaic outputs.
>

> ### 🧠 **Business Advisory Bot Features**
>
> Allow the bot to:
>
> * Offer **profit margin suggestions** (e.g., “Your price for Product X is too low based on cost.”)
> * Recommend **stock clearance** strategies for slow-moving items.
> * Forecast **stock-outs** using basic moving averages.
> * Recommend **bundles** or offers based on purchase patterns.
> * Summarize **monthly performance reports** in natural language.
>
> Sample queries it should handle:
>
> * “What’s the profit margin on pet collars?”
> * “How did I perform in June?”
> * “Is flea spray still in demand?”
> * “What’s my best-selling product this year?”
>
