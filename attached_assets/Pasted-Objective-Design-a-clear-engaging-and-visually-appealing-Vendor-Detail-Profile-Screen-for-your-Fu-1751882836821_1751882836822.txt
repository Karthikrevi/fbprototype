Objective:
Design a clear, engaging, and visually appealing Vendor Detail/Profile Screen for your FurrButler prototype. This screen is accessed when users select a vendor from the Vendor Listings Screen. It clearly showcases vendor details, services offered, pricing, booking availability, and lovable branding elements to foster trust and warmth.

📌 Design Guidelines:
Layout & Style:

Overall Feel: Clean, professional, yet warm and inviting.

Color Palette: Pastel backgrounds, clear contrasts, and consistent FurrButler brand accents.

Typography: Friendly, highly readable fonts like Inter or Poppins for clarity.

Visual Elements: Rounded cards or sections, subtle shadows, gentle borders, and charming pet icons for added warmth.

Branding & Visuals:

Vendor’s prominently displayed image/logo at the top (banner-style or clear header).

Clear use of pet-friendly visuals (paw prints, pet icons, heart symbols).

FurrButler branding subtly integrated (e.g., small logo or accent elements at the top or footer).

📱 Screen Components & Functionality:
Top Navigation/Header:

Sticky header containing:

Vendor Name prominently displayed.

Clearly visible back button/icon to navigate back to Vendor Listings.

Optional share/save button for users to bookmark or share vendor profile.

Vendor Main Information Section:

Large, appealing banner image or clearly visible logo/photo at the top.

Clearly readable Vendor Name.

Short, friendly description or tagline (e.g., “Your pet’s second favorite place after home!”).

Vendor Rating (star-based, e.g., ★★★★☆), clearly visible with numeric rating.

Vendor’s current availability status clearly indicated (e.g., "Open Now" in green).

Detailed Vendor Information Section:

Clearly structured information displayed in cards or neat sections:

Address: Full address with a clickable map link/icon (optional).

Contact Information: Clearly visible phone number or contact button (optional).

Working Hours: Clear, easy-to-read daily hours.

Distance from User: e.g., "Located just 2 km from you!"

Services & Pricing Section:

Clearly displayed services provided (e.g., grooming, boarding, bathing, training).

Individual pricing for each service clearly marked.

Lovable icons next to each service for quick visual understanding.

Clear Call-to-Action (CTA) button ("Book Now") prominently placed.

User Reviews Section (Recommended Optional Feature):

Lovable, scrollable section clearly showing user testimonials, star ratings, and comments.

Option for users to read more reviews via an expandable button.

✨ Lovable UX & Interaction Notes:
Gentle, subtle animations when scrolling or interacting with sections (e.g., card lift on hover, slight bounce).

Comforting microcopy encouraging users to book (e.g., "Charlie would love it here!").

Ensure ease of navigation and intuitive interactions.

🔍 Additional Functionality (Optional Enhancements):
Option to directly initiate chat or WhatsApp contact with vendors.

Easy-to-access vendor social media icons for quick credibility checks.

🧑‍💻 Expected Outcome (Prototype Screen):
An intuitive, fully interactive Vendor Detail/Profile Screen clearly connecting from the Vendor Listings, allowing seamless navigation toward booking functionality.
