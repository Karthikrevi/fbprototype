
# 🐾 **FurrButler Veterinary ERP – Phase 1 Detailed Development Prompt**

## 🎯 **Phase 1 Goal:**

Build a robust, professional, and intuitive Veterinary ERP core platform for FurrButler.
This foundational phase will focus strictly on providing vets and clinic staff with advanced clinical management, appointment scheduling, patient electronic medical records (EMR), and comprehensive billing functionalities.

---

## 🎨 **UI/UX Vision & Theme:**

**Overall Vibe**:

* **Professional & Clinical (inspired by Pantheon Veterinary ERP)**
* **Friendly & Inviting (subtle pet-themed accents)**
* Balance **clean efficiency** (clinic-focused, Pantheon-like UX/UI) with **warmth and empathy** (pet-parent-oriented, inviting).

**Color Palette**:

* Primary: Soft professional tones (white, soft grey, clinic blue/teal, pastel green).
* Accent: Warm, pet-friendly colors (soft orange, gentle yellow).
* Backgrounds: White and soft neutral shades ensuring readability and professional aesthetics.

**Typography**:

* Primary Text: Roboto/Open Sans (high readability).
* Headings/Titles: Nunito/Poppins (friendly, rounded fonts subtly indicating pet warmth).

**Iconography & Imagery**:

* Sleek, simple icons clearly illustrating functions (appointments, billing, records).
* Subtle playful pet elements (small paw prints, pet silhouettes) used sparingly to maintain professional clarity.

---

## 🚧 **Detailed Functional Requirements:**

### 📂 **1. Electronic Medical Records (EMR)**

Build a centralized, user-friendly medical record management system for pets.

**Key Features:**

* **Patient Registration:**

  * Easy input forms capturing detailed pet data: Name, Breed, Age, Gender, Weight, Microchip ID, Vaccination history, Allergies, Medical conditions, Owner information.
  * Robust data validation (error highlighting, auto-correction suggestions).

* **Digital Patient Records:**

  * Full medical history overview (chronological with filter/search capabilities).
  * Categorization of visits (vaccination, injury, illness, general checkup).
  * Image upload capability (X-rays, medical scans, pet photos).

* **Smart Templates & Notes:**

  * Pre-built templates (e.g., vaccination, wellness checks, surgeries) to speed up vet workflows.
  * SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) note-taking system, clearly defined and organized.

* **Advanced Search & Filters:**

  * Quick patient lookup via name, microchip, owner details, medical conditions.
  * Filter records by date, type of visit, vet, or treatment administered.

---

### 📆 **2. Advanced Appointment Scheduling System**

Deliver an intuitive, powerful scheduling module that enhances clinic productivity and client satisfaction.

**Key Features:**

* **Multi-Doctor Scheduling Dashboard:**

  * Interactive, color-coded appointment calendar (daily, weekly, monthly views).
  * Drag-and-drop appointment adjustments (rescheduling/cancellations).

* **Appointment Types & Duration:**

  * Customizable appointment lengths based on service (vaccination, surgery, consultation, grooming).
  * Predefined appointment slots for easy scheduling.

* **Automated Reminders & Communication:**

  * SMS, WhatsApp, Email integration for appointment confirmations, reminders, cancellations.
  * Customizable communication templates (friendly, professional wording).

* **Waitlist & Cancellation Management:**

  * Intelligent waitlist management (auto-fills canceled slots).
  * Real-time notifications for available openings.

---

### 💳 **3. Integrated Billing, Invoicing & Payments**

Develop an accurate, efficient, and secure billing and payments management module.

**Key Features:**

* **Auto-Invoicing & Billing:**

  * Generate professional digital invoices automatically after appointments.
  * Capability to manually edit or customize invoices as needed.

* **Integrated Payment Gateway:**

  * Multiple payment options (UPI, cards, mobile wallets, net banking).
  * Secure payment transactions with instant digital receipts.

* **Detailed Financial Records:**

  * Transparent financial reports (daily/weekly/monthly revenue, pending payments).
  * Comprehensive financial dashboard displaying billing trends, summaries.

---

## 🖥️ **Dashboard & User Experience:**

**Vet Dashboard:**

* Clean, professional sidebar navigation (Appointments, Patient Records, Billing, Reports).
* Dashboard widgets displaying daily appointments, pending tasks, billing summary, critical alerts (low inventory or unpaid bills).

**Notifications & Alerts:**

* Real-time alerts for upcoming appointments, inventory status, and critical patient follow-ups.
* Pop-up and banner notifications (non-intrusive, easily dismissible).

**Mobile Responsiveness:**

* Fully responsive design ensuring seamless access on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

---

## 🌟 **Core Technical Considerations:**

* **Backend Technology:**

  * Flask/Python-driven REST API endpoints.
  * Secure SQL databases (SQLite for MVP, scalable to PostgreSQL later).

* **Frontend Technology:**

  * HTML/CSS/JS with Bootstrap or TailwindCSS.
  * Dynamic UI components (React or Vue.js optional, depending on scalability plans).

* **Security & Compliance:**

  * Data encryption at rest and in transit (SSL certificates).
  * Strict adherence to data protection regulations (GDPR, data privacy, and pet parent confidentiality).

* **Performance:**

  * Optimization for fast loading and quick response.
  * Efficient backend queries for large datasets.

---

## 🎖 **Professional-Grade Finishing Touches:**

* Loading animations (subtle, professional yet playful).
* Hover effects and interactive transitions for buttons and links.
* Consistent spacing, padding, and margin for a visually balanced UI.
