Expand the ERP’s clinical foundations built in Phase 1 by adding a powerful veterinary inventory management module, pharmacy management, client relationship management (CRM), and integrated vendor management.
This phase will strengthen the ERP’s back-end operational capability, enabling veterinary clinics to manage their stocks efficiently, dispense medications professionally, enhance client interactions, and automate vendor relationships.

## 🚧 **Detailed Functional Requirements:**

## 📦 **1. Advanced Veterinary Inventory Management**

Develop a powerful, streamlined system to optimize inventory management across veterinary practices.

**Key Features:**

* **Real-time Stock Management:**

  * Dynamic inventory tracking (consumables, medications, equipment, supplies).
  * Barcode & QR code scanning capability for fast, accurate stock management (integration via smartphone cameras for ease of use).

* **Automated Reordering & Alerts:**

  * Auto-trigger reorder alerts based on user-defined stock thresholds.
  * Predictive stock management analytics (AI-assisted forecasting of inventory needs).

* **Multi-Location Inventory Support:**

  * Seamless management for clinics with multiple locations/branches.
  * Real-time visibility and transfers across branches.

* **Inventory Analytics & Reporting:**

  * Comprehensive analytics dashboard with interactive visuals (stock levels, movement, turnover rates, slow/fast-moving items).
  * Automated reporting: weekly/monthly stock reports, audits, discrepancies, and inventory valuation.

---

## 💊 **2. Integrated Pharmacy Management**

Establish a robust veterinary pharmacy management module integrated seamlessly with inventory and EMR.

**Key Features:**

* **Prescription Management:**

  * Digital prescriptions created directly from the EMR system.
  * Automatic cross-checking of medication dosages, interactions, and contraindications.

* **Dispensation Tracking:**

  * Accurate dispensation records linked directly to patient medical records and inventory.
  * Automated stock updates upon medication dispensing.

* **Pharmacy Compliance & Audit Logs:**

  * Complete digital logs of all dispensed medications, batch numbers, expiry dates, and pharmacist/vet signatures.
  * Compliance-ready reports for regulatory audits.

---

## 🤝 **3. Client Relationship Management (CRM)**

Implement a client-focused CRM module enhancing veterinary-client relationships and pet parent engagement.

**Key Features:**

* **Detailed Client Profiles:**

  * Client contact history, pet medical history, preferences, and detailed notes accessible instantly.
  * Pet-parent communication logs integrated (emails, calls, messages, appointment history).

* **Automated Follow-ups & Reminders:**

  * Customizable auto-reminders for check-ups, vaccinations, appointments, medication refills via WhatsApp, SMS, email.
  * Proactive client engagement through educational materials and pet-care recommendations.

* **Client Feedback & Satisfaction Tracking:**

  * Built-in client satisfaction surveys, automated feedback gathering post-visits.
  * Visual feedback analytics dashboard.

---

## 🛒 **4. Vendor & Supplier Management**

Establish seamless vendor integration and automated supply-chain interactions.

**Key Features:**

* **Vendor Profiles & Catalogs:**

  * Digitized vendor database with detailed vendor profiles, product catalogs, pricing, and contract terms.
  * Easy onboarding and integration of preferred veterinary suppliers.

* **Automated Procurement & Ordering:**

  * Direct order placements from inventory alerts, linked automatically to vendor profiles.
  * Digital tracking of order status, delivery timelines, and receipt verification.

* **Vendor Performance Tracking:**

  * Dashboards providing insights into vendor reliability, pricing trends, historical procurement data, and supply chain analytics.
  * Automated alerts on vendor contract renewals, changes in terms, pricing variations.

---

## 🌟 **Technical Requirements & Considerations:**

* **Backend Technology Enhancements:**

  * Flask REST API endpoints extended to handle new complex modules.
  * Scalable database solutions to handle larger datasets and multi-location deployments.

* **Frontend Technology Enhancements:**

  * Interactive JavaScript libraries (Chart.js, ApexCharts, or similar) for visual analytics.
  * Enhanced React/Vue.js components (optional based on future scaling).

* **Data Security & Privacy Compliance:**

  * Comprehensive logs and audit trails for every transaction.

* **Performance Optimization:**

  * Scalable architecture with optimized database queries.
  * Rapid load speeds, responsive UI across multiple devices and browsers.
