PART 1 — Shared infrastructure:

Create templates/privacy_policy.html:
Clean white professional page
FurrButler header
Sections:
1. Who we are and contact details
2. What personal data we collect:
   - Pet parents: name, email, phone,
     pet information, location,
     booking history, chat messages
   - Vendors: business name, email,
     phone, financial records,
     customer data, employee data
   - Vets: clinic name, email,
     patient records (medical data
     processed under Article 9 GDPR
     with explicit consent)
   - Handlers: name, email, phone,
     travel and passport data
   - NGOs: organisation name,
     contact details, stray records
3. Legal basis for processing:
   - Contract performance
   - Legitimate interests
   - Legal obligation
   - Consent (where stated)
4. How long we keep your data:
   - Account data: until deletion
     requested + 30 days
   - Booking records: 7 years
     (legal requirement)
   - Medical records: 10 years
     (medical legal requirement)
   - Chat messages: 2 years
   - Financial records: 7 years
     (tax legal requirement)
5. Who we share data with:
   - We do not sell your data
   - Razorpay for payment processing
     (when activated)
   - SendGrid for email delivery
   - No other third parties
6. International transfers:
   - Data stored in Replit servers
   - Standard contractual clauses apply
7. Your rights:
   - Right to access your data
   - Right to rectification
   - Right to erasure (with exceptions
     for legal retention requirements)
   - Right to data portability
   - Right to object to processing
   - Right to withdraw consent
8. How to exercise your rights:
   - Email: privacy@furrbutler.com
   - Or use the in-app data tools
9. Cookie policy:
   - We use essential cookies only
   - No tracking or advertising cookies
   - Session cookie for login only
10. Complaints:
    You have the right to complain
    to your local data protection
    authority
Last updated: current date

PART 8 — React Native API endpoints:

Add these JSON API routes for React:

POST /api/v1/gdpr/consent:
Body: required_consent,
      marketing_consent,
      data_sharing_consent
Requires token
Save to Replit db
Return: { success: true }

GET /api/v1/gdpr/download-data:
Requires token
Return all user data as JSON

POST /api/v1/gdpr/delete-account:
Requires token
Schedule deletion
Return: { success, deletion_date }

GET /api/v1/gdpr/cancel-deletion:
Requires token
Cancel scheduled deletion
Return: { success }

POST /api/v1/gdpr/update-preferences:
Requires token
Body: marketing_consent,
      data_sharing_consent
Update in Replit db
Return: { success }
