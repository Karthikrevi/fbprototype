Clinical Staff Management at
/furrvet/staff-management

/furrvet/staff-management GET:

Pull any certifications expiring
  within 60 days

Weekly schedule view

Tab 3 — Payroll:
Same as vendor payroll
But uses furrvet staff table

Generate payslips same logic

Tab 4 — Certifications:
List all staff certifications

PART 2 — Clinical CRM:

The vendor ERP has CRM at /erp/crm
For FurrVet adapt as
Client Relations at /furrvet/clients

This manages pet OWNER relationships
not the pets themselves (those are
in patients)

Add routes to furrvet.py:

/furrvet/clients GET:
Pull all pet_owners from furrvet.db
Show owner list with:
  Owner name, phone, email
  Number of pets
  Last visit date
  Total spend (from invoices)
  Communication preferences

/furrvet/clients/<int:owner_id> GET:
Owner detail showing:
  All their pets with last visit
  Complete interaction history
  Invoice history and total spend
  Vaccination reminders due
  Upcoming appointments
  Communication log

/furrvet/clients/reminders GET:
All client_reminders from furrvet.db
Filter: pending, sent, overdue
For each reminder show:
  Pet name, owner name
  Reminder type (vaccination due,
  checkup, medication refill)
  Due date
  Mark Sent button
  Send via WhatsApp button
    (Coming Soon badge)

/furrvet/clients/campaigns GET and POST:
Vaccination reminder campaigns:
  Select: All dogs due for rabies
  Select: All cats due for FVRCP
  Select: All patients not seen
    in 6 months
  Generate reminder list
  Send reminders (manual first,
  WhatsApp when activated)

PART 3 — Clinical Accounting:

The vendor ERP has full accounting
at /erp/reports
For FurrVet adapt as
Clinic Financials at /furrvet/financials

Add routes to furrvet.py:

/furrvet/financials GET:
Clinical finance dashboard showing:
  Today's revenue (from invoices)
  This month revenue
  Outstanding invoices (AR)
  Pending supplier payments (AP)
  Quick links to all reports

/furrvet/financials/pnl GET:
Pull from furrvet invoices table
for revenue
Pull clinic expenses (if tracked)
Show:
  Total revenue by service type:
    Consultations, Vaccinations,
    Lab Tests, Surgery, Pharmacy
  Total expenses
  Gross profit per category
  Net profit

/furrvet/financials/ar GET:
Accounts Receivable from invoices
where payment_status = pending
Group by:
  0-30 days: current
  31-60 days: overdue
  60+ days: seriously overdue
Show owner name, amount, days outstanding
Record Payment button per invoice
  → marks invoice as paid

/furrvet/financials/ap GET:
Supplier payments from furrvet.db
purchase_orders table
Outstanding orders
Record payment functionality

/furrvet/financials/gst GET:
GST from invoices
tax_amount collected
GST on supplier purchases
Net GST payable

/furrvet/financials/export/pnl GET:
Download P&L as CSV
