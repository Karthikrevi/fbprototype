In main.py and templates only, create the
Board Report page.
Do not modify any existing routes.
Do not delete any accounts or data.
Do not modify the Replit key-value db.

Route: /erp/finance/board-report GET

Pull this data for this vendor:
- Vendor business name, city, category
- Revenue: this month, last month,
  last 6 months monthly breakdown
- Top 5 products by revenue
- Top 3 services by booking count
- Expense breakdown by category
- Gross margin percentage
- Net profit this month and last month
- Customer count from crm_customers
- New customers this month
- Booking count this month vs last month
- Average order value
- Inventory total value and SKU count
- Low stock items count
- Outstanding receivables amount
- Outstanding payables amount
- Employee count and monthly payroll cost

Pass all data to board_report.html
