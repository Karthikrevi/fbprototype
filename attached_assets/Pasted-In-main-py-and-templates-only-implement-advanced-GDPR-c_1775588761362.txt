PART 1 — FurrVet Medical Data:

Create templates/furrvet_consent.html:
Explicit consent form for medical
data processing under GDPR Article 9.

Must contain:
Header: Medical Data Processing Consent
Subheader: FurrVet — Veterinary Records

Explanation section:
"FurrVet processes the following
special category data under
GDPR Article 9(2)(h) — processing
necessary for medical diagnosis,
treatment and veterinary care:

- Pet health records and diagnoses
- Vaccination and treatment history
- Laboratory test results
- Surgical and procedure records
- Prescribed medications
- Medical imaging and documents
- Hospitalization records"

Legal basis section:
"This data is processed under:
- Article 9(2)(h): Medical diagnosis
  and treatment
- Article 9(2)(a): Explicit consent
  where required
- Legal obligation to maintain
  veterinary records"

Retention notice:
"Medical records are retained for
a minimum of 10 years as required
by veterinary medical law. Records
cannot be deleted during this
retention period even upon request."

Data controller notice:
"Your veterinary clinic is the
data controller for all patient
records. FurrVet acts as a data
processor on behalf of your clinic."

Required explicit consents:
☐ I explicitly consent to FurrVet
  processing pet medical records
  including diagnoses, treatments,
  and health history (required)

☐ I understand that medical records
  cannot be deleted for 10 years
  as required by law (required)

☐ I consent to medical records
  being shared with referral vets
  when necessary for treatment
  (optional but recommended)

☐ I consent to anonymised data
  being used for veterinary
  research purposes (optional)

Save All Consents button
POSTs to /furrvet/gdpr/consent

Add route /furrvet/gdpr/consent POST:
Save all consent values to
furrvet_gdpr_consents table:

CREATE TABLE IF NOT EXISTS
furrvet_gdpr_consents (
    id INTEGER PRIMARY KEY AUTOINCREMENT,
    vet_id INTEGER,
    medical_processing_consent BOOLEAN,
    retention_acknowledged BOOLEAN,
    referral_sharing_consent BOOLEAN,
    research_consent BOOLEAN,
    consent_date TEXT,
    consent_version TEXT DEFAULT '1.0',
    ip_address TEXT,
    created_at TEXT DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP
)

Add route /furrvet/gdpr/privacy-notice GET:
Render furrvet_privacy.html

Create templates/furrvet_privacy.html:
Professional white design
Navy blue headers

Sections:
1. Medical Data Controller Information
   Clinic name and contact as controller
   FurrButler as data processor

2. Special Category Data We Process:
   Full list of medical data types
   Legal basis for each type

3. Retention Schedule:
   Active records: indefinitely
   Inactive records: 10 years minimum
   After 10 years: anonymised
   Cannot be deleted on request
   during retention period

4. Who Accesses Medical Records:
   Your veterinary clinic staff
   FurrButler technical team
   (limited, for system maintenance)
   Referral vets (with consent)
   Regulatory authorities (if required)

5. Patient Rights with Medical Exceptions:
   Right to access: YES — full access
   Right to rectification: YES
   Right to erasure: LIMITED
   Cannot erase during 10 year
   retention period
   Right to portability: YES
   Can export records at any time
   Right to object: LIMITED
   Cannot object to legally required
   processing

6. Data Security:
   Encrypted storage
   Access logging
   Role-based access control
   Regular security audits

7. Cross-border Transfers:
   Records stored in Replit servers
   Standard contractual clauses apply
   Adequacy decisions referenced

8. Complaints:
   Contact your national data
   protection authority
   In India: contact MeitY

PART 2 — FurrWings International
Data Transfers:

Create templates/furrwings_privacy.html:
International data transfer notice

Sections:
1. International Transfer Notice:
   "FurrWings facilitates international
   pet travel. As part of this service,
   your pet's health and passport data
   may be transferred to and processed
   in countries outside India and the
   European Economic Area."

2. Countries We Transfer Data To:
   "Data is transferred to destination
   country veterinary and customs
   authorities as required by law.
   The specific countries depend on
   your pet's travel destination."

3. Legal Basis for Transfer:
   "Transfers are made under:
   - Article 49(1)(b): Transfer
     necessary for performance
     of contract
   - Legal obligation to destination
     country authorities
   - Standard contractual clauses
     where applicable"

4. What Data Is Transferred:
   Pet passport information
   Vaccination records
   Health certificates
   Owner contact details
   Travel documentation

5. Your Rights:
   You can request details of
   specific transfers made for
   your pet's travel
   Contact: privacy@furrbutler.com

6. Data Retention:
   Travel records: 7 years
   Pet passport: life of pet + 5 years
   Health certificates: 5 years

PART 3 — Data Breach Response:

Add route /admin/gdpr/breach-log GET:
Admin only
Show all entries in data_breach_log table
Created in Phase 1

Add route /admin/gdpr/breach-log POST:
Admin only
Add new breach record
Fields:
- detected_date
- description
- affected_users (count or description)
- severity: low/medium/high/critical
- reported_to_authority boolean
- report_date
- resolution description

Create templates/admin_breach_log.html:
Clean professional admin page
Table of all breach records
Add New Breach button
For each breach show:
- Date detected
- Severity badge
- Affected users
- Reported to authority status
- Resolution status

Add note:
"Under GDPR Article 33, data breaches
must be reported to the supervisory
authority within 72 hours of detection
if they pose a risk to individuals.
In India report to MeitY."
