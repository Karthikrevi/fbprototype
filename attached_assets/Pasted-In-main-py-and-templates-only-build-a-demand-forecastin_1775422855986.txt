In main.py and templates only, build a 
demand forecasting system for the vendor 
inventory module.

WHAT IT NEEDS TO DO:

Create a function called forecast_demand 
that takes vendor_id and product_id.

It must:
- Pull last 12 months of monthly sales 
  from sales_log for that product
- Use numpy linear regression to calculate
  trend and forecast for 30, 60 and 90 days
- Detect seasonality by comparing current 
  month sales to the same month last year
  and applying a seasonal adjustment factor
- Calculate days until stockout based on 
  current stock and average daily sales
- Suggest a reorder quantity to cover 
  60 days of forecasted demand
- Classify trend as growing, stable or declining
- Classify confidence as high, medium or low
  based on how many months of data exist
- Flag dead stock if no sales in last 60 days
- Flag seasonal warning if this month 
  historically runs significantly higher 
  or lower than average
- Return insufficient_data if less than 
  2 months of sales history exists

Create a route /erp/inventory/forecast that:
- Runs forecast_demand for every product 
  belonging to the logged in vendor
- Sorts results by urgency — products 
  running out soonest shown first
- Separates products into groups:
  needs reorder now, dead stock, 
  no data available, everything else
- Passes all results to the template

Create template inventory_forecast.html that:
- Shows a summary at the top with stat cards:
  total products, needs reorder count,
  dead stock count, no data count
- Shows a prominent red panel for any 
  products that will run out within 30 days
- Shows a card per product with:
  current stock, trend badge, confidence badge,
  seasonal warning badge if applicable,
  dead stock warning if applicable,
  forecast for 30/60/90 days with 
  upper and lower bounds,
  days until stockout,
  suggested reorder quantity
- Shows a small Chart.js line chart per product
  with last 12 months actual sales as a solid line
  and the 30 day forecast as a dashed point
- Shows dead stock panel for products 
  with no recent sales
- Shows no data panel for products with 
  insufficient history
- Has a prominent disclaimer box that says:
  "These forecasts are generated using 
  historical sales data and statistical models.
  Actual demand may vary due to market conditions,
  seasonality, promotions or other factors.
  FurrButler provides these forecasts as a 
  planning aid only and is not responsible 
  for business decisions made based on 
  this information. Always apply your own 
  judgement."
  Style it as a neutral grey info box
  Place it at the bottom of the page
- Back button links explicitly to /erp/inventory
  Do not use history.back()

In inventory_management.html only:
Update the Forecast button in each table row
and the Demand Forecast button at the top
to both link to /erp/inventory/forecast

Do not modify any other routes.
Do not modify any other templates
except inventory_management.html 
for the button links only.
Do not modify any database tables.
Do not delete any accounts or data.
Do not modify the Replit key-value db.
