Special Category Data Processing

FurrVet processes the following special category data under GDPR Article 9(2)(h) — processing necessary for medical diagnosis, treatment and veterinary care:

  • Pet health records and diagnoses
  • Vaccination and treatment history
  • Laboratory test results
  • Surgical and procedure records
  • Prescribed medications
  • Medical imaging and documents
  • Hospitalization records

Legal Basis

This data is processed under:

  • Article 9(2)(h): Medical diagnosis and treatment
  • Article 9(2)(a): Explicit consent where required
  • Legal obligation to maintain veterinary records

Retention Notice

Medical records are retained for a minimum of 10 years as required by veterinary medical law. Records cannot be deleted during this retention period even upon request.

Data Controller Notice

Your veterinary clinic is the data controller for all patient records. FurrVet acts as a data processor on behalf of your clinic.
