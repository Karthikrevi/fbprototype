🐾 FurrButler Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

1. Who We Are

FurrButler is a comprehensive pet services platform providing grooming, boarding, veterinary, marketplace, and pet travel services. For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@furrbutler.com.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

Pet Parents: Name, email address, phone number, pet information (name, breed, species, medical history), location data, booking history, order history, and chat messages.

Vendors: Business name, email address, phone number, business address, financial records, customer interaction data, and employee data.

Veterinarians: Clinic name, email address, patient records including medical data. Medical data is processed under GDPR Article 9 with explicit consent as it constitutes special category data.

Handlers: Name, email address, phone number, travel certifications, and pet passport/travel data.

NGOs: Organisation name, contact details, staff information, and stray animal records.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to fulfil our service agreements with you (bookings, orders, travel arrangements).
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our services, preventing fraud, and ensuring platform security.
  • Legal obligation: Retaining financial and medical records as required by law.
  • Consent: Marketing communications and optional data sharing, which you can withdraw at any time.

4. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Account data: Until deletion is requested, plus 30 days grace period.
  • Booking records: 7 years (legal requirement).
  • Medical records: 10 years (medical legal requirement).
  • Chat messages: 2 years from date of message.
  • Financial records: 7 years (tax legal requirement).

5. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your data.

  • Razorpay: For payment processing (when activated).
  • SendGrid: For email delivery.
  • No other third parties receive your personal data.

6. International Transfers

Your data is stored on Replit servers. Where data is transferred internationally, standard contractual clauses apply to ensure adequate protection.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws including GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us.
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data.
  • Erase your personal data (with exceptions for legal retention requirements).
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object to processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

8. How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise your rights by:

  • Emailing us at privacy@furrbutler.com
  • Using the in-app data tools in your Settings page (Download My Data, Delete My Account)

9. Cookie Policy

We use essential cookies only to keep you logged in. We do not use any tracking or advertising cookies. The only cookie set is a session cookie for login purposes.

10. Complaints

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.
