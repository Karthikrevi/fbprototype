FurrWings International Data Transfer Notice

Information about cross-border data transfers for pet travel

1. International Transfer Notice

FurrWings facilitates international pet travel. As part of this service, your pet's health and passport data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside India and the European Economic Area.

2. Countries We Transfer Data To

Data is transferred to destination country veterinary and customs authorities as required by law. The specific countries depend on your pet's travel destination.

Each destination country may have its own data protection laws. Where the destination country does not have an adequacy decision from the European Commission, additional safeguards are applied.

3. Legal Basis for Transfer

Transfers are made under:

  • Article 49(1)(b): Transfer necessary for the performance of a contract between you and FurrButler for international pet travel services
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with destination country veterinary and customs requirements
  • Standard contractual clauses: Applied where necessary to ensure adequate protection

Data CategoryPurpose

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request details of specific transfers made for your pet's travel
  • Obtain copies of any safeguards applied to your data transfers
  • Withdraw consent for optional data sharing (note: this may prevent travel services from being provided)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

For enquiries about international data transfers, contact: privacy@furrbutler.com
