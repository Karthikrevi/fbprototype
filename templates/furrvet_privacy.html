FurrVet Medical Data Privacy Notice

Special category data processing under GDPR

1. Medical Data Controller Information

Data Controller: Your veterinary clinic (the clinic using FurrVet)

The clinic is responsible for determining the purposes and means of processing patient medical data.

Data Processor: FurrButler (operating the FurrVet platform)

FurrButler processes medical data solely on behalf of and under the instructions of the veterinary clinic. Contact: privacy@furrbutler.com

3. Retention Schedule

Record TypeRetention Period
Active patient recordsRetained indefinitely while patient is active
Inactive patient recordsMinimum 10 years from last treatment
After 10-year retention periodAnonymised for research or securely deleted
Important: Medical records cannot be deleted on request during the mandatory 10-year retention period. This retention is required by veterinary medical law and supersedes the right to erasure under GDPR Article 17(3)(b).

4. Who Accesses Medical Records

  • Your veterinary clinic staff — Full access as data controller
  • FurrButler technical team — Limited access for system maintenance only, under strict confidentiality obligations
  • Referral veterinarians — With explicit consent from the clinic, when necessary for treatment continuity
  • Regulatory authorities — If required by law or court order

5. Patient Rights with Medical Exceptions

RightAvailabilityNotes

Right to ErasureLIMITEDCannot erase records during the mandatory 10-year retention period (Article 17(3)(b))

Right to ObjectLIMITEDCannot object to processing that is legally required for veterinary record-keeping

6. Data Security

  • Encrypted storage: All medical data is stored with encryption at rest
  • Access logging: All access to patient records is logged with timestamp, user, and action
  • Role-based access control: Only authorised clinic staff can access patient records
  • Regular security audits: Periodic review of access controls and security measures

7. Cross-border Transfers

Medical records are stored on Replit servers. Where data is transferred internationally:

  • Standard contractual clauses (SCCs) apply to ensure adequate protection
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission are referenced where applicable
  • Data is only transferred where necessary for the provision of the FurrVet service

8. Complaints

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you may:

  • Contact your veterinary clinic directly (as data controller)
  • Contact FurrButler at privacy@furrbutler.com
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority
  • In India: Contact the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)
  • In the EU: Contact your local Data Protection Authority
