🎯 Financial Management Best Practices

📋 Monthly Close Process
  • Review all transactions and reconcile accounts
  • Generate and review financial statements
  • Analyze variances and key performance indicators
  • Prepare management reports and board presentations
💰 Cash Management
  • Monitor cash flow and liquidity positions daily
  • Optimize payment timing and collection processes
  • Maintain appropriate cash reserves
  • Use forecasting to anticipate funding needs
📊 Financial Analysis
  • Track key financial ratios and metrics
  • Perform trend analysis and benchmarking
  • Conduct scenario planning and sensitivity analysis
  • Monitor budget vs. actual performance
🔒 Compliance & Controls
  • Maintain proper internal controls and segregation
  • Ensure compliance with accounting standards
  • Keep detailed audit trails for all transactions
  • Regular review and update of financial policies
